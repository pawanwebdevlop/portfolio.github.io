About Me

Hello, I am Pawan.

As a designer and developer, I understand the perfect user interface should look good and work even better. Alongside my clients, I uncover problems and solve them. I mostly focus on simplicity, ergonomics, responsive design, cross-browser compatibility and SEO-friendly code. I am currently working as a freelancer.

  • Web Design

    My design philosophy is simple. I always place my client’s customers at the heart of the user experience - resulting in well-designed, user-friendly websites that meets the client’s objectives.

  • Web Development

    I have vast experience in back-end web technologies such as PHP and MySQL to create multi-functional web portals - from content management systems to full-fledged e-commerce solutions, and beyond.

  • WordPress

    I build Custom WordPress Development solutions that is secured, optimised for SEO, user focused and guaranteed to generate better ROI for your business in longer run. My main priorities for WordPress Development include security, User Experience and conversions and developing with WordPress core minimising usage of plugins.

  • Responsive Design

    Websites are nowadays viewed on multiple devices. With my skills in front-end web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3 , Bootstrap and Javascript, I can create a flexible, responsive site that looks good on all screen sizes.
